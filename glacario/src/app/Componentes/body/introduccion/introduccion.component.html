This Glossary is intended to assist new collaborators attending the gA Academy, encouraging them to use technical acronyms, concepts and business savvy lingo. The definitions represent the meanings understood and shared by the majority of the participants of this project.


We, gA, are a Latin-American Organization that operates globally; we create digital environments, innovate and transform businesses. With that always in mind, we will have a heavy use of terms borrowed from fields of study such as IT, technology development , business improvement and methodologies . As it would be impossible to incorporate full glossaries of these sciences, only some of the more commonly-used terms are included.


The entries are fully cross-referenced. Headwords appear in bold face and words within the definition which also appear in this type style are defined elsewhere in their most common form.


Standard English spelling is used throughout, except in those cases where Spanish is more generally used.


For additional definitions of terms borrowed from other disciplines, the reader is encouraged to consult specialized dictionaries
